MISSION AND VISION

Digital Lending Platform for Banking Products in Bangladesh

“LinkedSage Limited” is a private limited company owned and managed by a team of highly skilled professionals and technical experts, having diversified experience in innovative technology based solutions. Linkedsage operates based on the principle of service excellence through consistency, accuracy, reliability and timely delivery of innovative digital solutions. Despite the rapid advancement in technology and having achieved significant progress in financial inclusion over the past years, there is still a large segment of the population especially Small and Medium Enterprise (SME) along with startups, who are being deprived from availing loan and other financial facilities.

1. Vision and value proposition

Bearing this simple market need in mind, our team of experts have developed a platform named “LoanerBazar” to bridge this gap between the lender and the borrower with the view to facilitate financial inclusion by making loans easily available for these customers. The platform will enable the borrower to apply for a loan digitally and LoanerBazar will act as the mediator to connect such loans with respective lenders by matching requirements from Banks, FI’s and Microcredit service providers.

Our integrated platform will facilitate the lending and borrowing process, making it easy to apply for a loan at the customers fingertips.
